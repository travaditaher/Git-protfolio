Face Recognition and Face-Mask Detection System

  • Tech Stack: Python,Keras, OpenCV2, Tkinter, Numpy
  • Codebase: Git Repo

In this innovative project, my team collectively led the development of a cutting-edge Face Recognition and Face-Mask Detection system. Leveraging state-of-the-art Machine Learning technologies such as Tensorflow, Keras, and OpenCV, we implemented a sophisticated ML model integrated with a user-friendly interface based on the pre-built MobileNetV2 neural network architecture.

  • Engineered a robust ML model capable of recognizing faces and detecting the presence or absence of face masks, contributing to enhanced public safety measures.
  • Introduced a penalty system for individuals without face masks, providing a versatile solution to enforce safety protocols in various settings.
  • Achieved an 'O' grade, reflecting the project's outstanding performance and adherence to high standards of excellence.
  • Simultaneously, our project was recognized in academia, leading to the publication of a research paper in the International Research Journal of Engineering and Technology (IRJET).

This project not only addresses the critical need for advanced safety measures but also stands out for its academic contribution, reinforcing our commitment to excellence in both practical application and scholarly endeavors.
