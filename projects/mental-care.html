The Mental Care WEB APPLICATION

  • Tech Stack: JavaScript, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, Spring-boot
  • Codebase: Git Repo

In a joint effort, we embarked on a journey to create a meaningful digital space using cutting-edge technologies. Our collaborative project leveraged the power of the Spring Boot framework, MySQL, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build a robust and compassionate website.

The primary aim of our endeavor was to establish a seamless connection between psychiatrists and individuals dealing with Mental Health Illness. Recognizing the importance of accessibility and user experience, we meticulously developed a user-friendly interface that prioritizes the well-being of our users.

  • Integrating the Spring Boot framework provided a solid foundation, ensuring scalability and efficiency. The utilization of MySQL as our database management system allowed for secure and organized storage of critical data.
  • Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript were instrumental in crafting an aesthetically pleasing and responsive interface. Our commitment to a clean and intuitive design ensures that users, including patients and psychiatrists, have a seamless and enjoyable experience.
  • We focused on empowering patients by enabling them to register and schedule online treatment appointments effortlessly. The user-friendly interface simplifies the process, fostering a sense of control and comfort for individuals seeking mental health support.

In essence, our collaborative effort resulted in the creation of a purpose-driven platform that transcends technological boundaries. By bridging the gap between mental health professionals and those in need, our project strives to make a positive impact on the well-being of individuals in our community. Together, we believe in the potential of technology to bring about meaningful change, one appointment at a time.
